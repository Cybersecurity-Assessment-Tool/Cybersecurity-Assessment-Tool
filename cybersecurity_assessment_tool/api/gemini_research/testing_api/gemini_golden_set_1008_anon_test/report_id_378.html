Recommendations

The following actionable recommendations are provided to mitigate identified risks and enhance the overall cybersecurity posture of [Organization Name].

  1. Implement MFA for Email:

    • Action: Urgently enable and enforce Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all email accounts across the organization.

    • Benefit: This will significantly reduce the risk of unauthorized access to email accounts, even if passwords are compromised, protecting against phishing, credential stuffing, and business email compromise (BEC) attacks.

    • Priority: Immediate / High

  2. Verify Unencrypted Web Server Risk:

    • Action: Conduct a thorough investigation to confirm the current status of the "Unencrypted Web Server" risk (Port 80).

    • Details:

      • Determine if the service was indeed decommissioned or moved, aligning with the scan results.

      • If the service is still active and potentially exposed (perhaps on a different IP or port not covered by the scan), take immediate steps to secure it with HTTPS/SSL/TLS or decommission it if no longer required.

      • Update the organization’s risk register based on the findings.

    • Benefit: Ensures that all identified vulnerabilities are accurately tracked and addressed, preventing potential exposure from outdated risk information.

    • Priority: High

  3. Comprehensive Scan Coverage (Future):

    • Action: Ensure that future network scans comprehensively cover all relevant internal and external assets, including all known IP ranges and domains.

    • Benefit: Provides a complete and accurate picture of the organization’s attack surface, minimizing the chance of undiscovered vulnerabilities.

    • Priority: Medium
