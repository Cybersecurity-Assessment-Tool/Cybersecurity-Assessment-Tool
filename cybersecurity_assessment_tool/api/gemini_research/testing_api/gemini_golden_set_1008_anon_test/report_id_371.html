Key Gaps Identified from Questionnaire:

The "No" responses highlight critical areas of weakness that significantly elevate the organization’s risk profile:

  • Lack of MFA for Email Access: This is a critical gap. Email accounts are frequent targets for attackers and often serve as gateways to other systems. Without MFA, a simple password compromise can lead to widespread unauthorized access.

  • Lack of MFA for Sensitive Data Systems: Another critical gap. Direct access to sensitive data systems without MFA leaves critical information vulnerable to compromise if primary credentials are stolen or guessed.

  • No Annual Security Awareness Training for All Employees: This is a high risk. Cybersecurity threats constantly evolve. Without regular, updated training, employees’ knowledge and vigilance can degrade over time, increasing the likelihood of successful attacks.
