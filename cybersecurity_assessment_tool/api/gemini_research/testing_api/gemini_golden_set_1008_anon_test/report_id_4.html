Risk Assessment

Based on the review of organizational controls and technical scans, the following risks have been identified:

Risk Name Overview Severity

Recommendations

The following recommendations are provided to mitigate the identified risks and improve the overall cybersecurity posture of [Organization Name]:

  • Implement MFA for Email Access:

    • Action: Mandate and rigorously enforce Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all employee email accounts without exception. This should be considered a critical security priority due to the high risk associated with email account compromise. Consider extending MFA to all cloud-based services and critical internal systems.

    • Benefit: Significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access to email accounts and other services, even if passwords are stolen or compromised through phishing or other means.

  • Establish Comprehensive Annual Security Awareness Training:

    • Action: Develop and implement a mandatory, comprehensive annual security awareness training program for all employees across the organization. This training should be recurring and tailored to current threat landscapes.

    • Topics to Cover: The training should include modules on identifying and reporting phishing attempts, social engineering tactics, strong password hygiene, recognizing malicious links and attachments, secure browsing habits, and clear incident reporting procedures.

    • Benefit: Enhances employee vigilance, reduces the likelihood of successful human-factor attacks, and fosters a robust security-conscious culture within the organization.
