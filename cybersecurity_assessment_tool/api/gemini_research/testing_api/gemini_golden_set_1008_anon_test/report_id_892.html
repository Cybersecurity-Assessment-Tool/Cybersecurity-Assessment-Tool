Recommendations

To mitigate the identified risks and strengthen the overall cybersecurity posture of [Organization Name], the following actionable recommendations are provided:

  • Implement Mandatory MFA for Email Access:

    • Enable and enforce Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all user accounts accessing email services. This should be considered a top priority given the criticality of email in business operations and as a common vector for initial compromise.

    • Provide clear instructions and support for employees to enroll in and use MFA.

    • Consider utilizing hardware tokens or authenticator apps for stronger authentication methods.

  • Establish Mandatory Security Awareness Training for New Employees:

    • Develop and implement a mandatory cybersecurity awareness training program specifically for all new employees as part of their onboarding process. This training should occur before new hires are granted access to critical company systems.

    • The training content should cover essential topics such as phishing identification, password best practices, safe browsing habits, incident reporting procedures, and the organization’s acceptable use policy.

    • Reinforce the importance of security culture from the outset of their employment.

  • Continuous Security Control Review and Updates:

    • Regularly review and update existing security policies and controls to adapt to the evolving threat landscape and organizational changes.

    • Ensure that all security awareness training, including annual refreshers, is current and addresses the latest threats and best practices.

    • Conduct periodic internal audits of MFA implementation and security training completion rates.
