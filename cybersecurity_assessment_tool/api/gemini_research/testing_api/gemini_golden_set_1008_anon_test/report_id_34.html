6. Recommendations

To address the identified risks and enhance the overall cybersecurity posture of [Organization Name], the following recommendations are provided:

  1. Implement Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for Email Access:

    • Action: Immediately deploy and enforce MFA for all email accounts across the organization. This should be a mandatory control for all users.

    • Benefit: Significantly reduces the risk of credential theft and unauthorized access to email accounts, mitigating phishing and BEC attack vectors.

  2. Develop and Implement an Employee Acceptable Use Policy (AUP):

    • Action: Draft, formalize, and communicate a comprehensive AUP to all employees. Ensure employees acknowledge and understand the policy, with regular reviews and updates.

    • Benefit: Establishes clear guidelines for responsible use of company assets, reduces insider risks, minimizes legal exposure, and fosters a culture of security.

  3. Conduct Annual Security Awareness Training (SAT) for All Employees:

    • Action: Establish a program for mandatory annual (or more frequent) security awareness training for all employees, covering current threats, best practices, and organizational policies.

    • Benefit: Elevates the human firewall by ensuring employees are continuously educated about evolving cyber threats, improving their ability to identify and report suspicious activities.

  4. Conduct Comprehensive Technical Assessments:

    • Action: Given the limited scope of the provided scan data, it is recommended to conduct a full, authenticated internal and external vulnerability assessment, followed by penetration testing.

    • Benefit: Provides a deeper understanding of the organization’s technical attack surface and identifies exploitable vulnerabilities that might not be visible from a basic perimeter scan.
