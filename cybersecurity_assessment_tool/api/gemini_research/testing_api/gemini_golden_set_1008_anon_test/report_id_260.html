Recommendations

The following recommendations are provided to address the identified risks and improve the overall cybersecurity posture of [Organization Name].

  • Implement Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for Computer Logins:

    • Action: Immediately implement MFA for all computer logins across the organization. This significantly enhances protection against credential theft and unauthorized access.

    • Details: Evaluate and deploy suitable MFA solutions (e.g., authenticator apps, FIDO2 security keys, smart cards). Ensure a phased rollout with clear communication and user training. Develop clear policies for MFA enforcement and exception handling.

    • Priority: Critical

  • Investigate and Secure Open Port 80/tcp:

    • Action: Thoroughly investigate the service running on port 80/tcp on .

    • Details: Determine if the service is necessary for business operations. If so, ensure it is securely configured, patched to the latest version, and adequately protected (e.g., with a web application firewall (WAF), network firewalls, or strict access controls). If the service is not necessary, close the port immediately to reduce the attack surface. Document the purpose and configuration of all publicly exposed services.

    • Priority: High

  • Maintain and Regularly Review Acceptable Use Policy:

    • Action: Ensure the existing Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) is regularly reviewed, updated, and communicated.

    • Details: The AUP should reflect current technologies, threat landscapes, and organizational requirements. Regular communication ensures employees are aware of their responsibilities and expected conduct regarding company assets and data.

    • Priority: Medium

  • Continue and Enhance Security Awareness Training:

    • Action: Maintain the annual security awareness training program for all employees.

    • Details: Consider incorporating advanced topics, practical exercises (e.g., phishing simulations), and role-specific training to further enhance employee vigilance and response capabilities against evolving threats. Regularly assess the effectiveness of training.

    • Priority: Medium
