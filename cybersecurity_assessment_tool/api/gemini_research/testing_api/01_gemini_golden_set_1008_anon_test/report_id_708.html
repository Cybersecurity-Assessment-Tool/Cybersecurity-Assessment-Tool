Recommendations

Based on the findings, the following actions are recommended to mitigate the identified risks and improve the overall security posture of [Organization Name]. Recommendations are prioritized by severity.

  1. [CRITICAL] Implement MFA for Email (ORG-001):

    • Immediately enforce a policy requiring MFA for all user access to the email system (e.g., O365, Google Workspace).

    • Prioritize robust MFA methods such as authenticator apps or hardware tokens over less secure methods like SMS.

    • Conduct a review of all email accounts for signs of existing compromise.

  2. [HIGH] Establish Annual Security Training (ORG-002):

    • Procure and deploy a security awareness training platform.

    • Develop a mandatory annual training curriculum for all employees covering key topics like phishing, password security, and social engineering.

    • Track completion to ensure 100% compliance.

  3. [HIGH] Develop and Implement an AUP (ORG-003):

    • Draft a comprehensive Acceptable Use Policy that clearly defines the rules for using company networks, devices, and data.

    • Have the policy reviewed by legal and HR departments.

    • Require all current and new employees to read and formally acknowledge the policy.

  4. [HIGH] Remediate Scan Failures and Schedule Regular Scans (SYS-001):

    • Investigate and resolve the technical issues that prevented the network scan from completing successfully.

    • Establish a formal vulnerability management program that includes, at a minimum, quarterly external vulnerability scans.

    • Ensure a process is in place to review scan results and remediate identified vulnerabilities in a timely manner.
