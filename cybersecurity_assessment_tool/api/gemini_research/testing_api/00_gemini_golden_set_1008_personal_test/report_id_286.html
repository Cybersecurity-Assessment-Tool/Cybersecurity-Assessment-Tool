Recommendations

To mitigate the identified risks and enhance the overall cybersecurity posture of Terraform Global, the following recommendations are provided:

Immediate Actions (High Priority)

  • Implement MFA for Email Access: Deploy and enforce Multi-Factor Authentication for all user accounts accessing email services. This significantly reduces the risk of account compromise from stolen or phished credentials.

  • Implement MFA for Computer Login: Implement MFA for logging into all company computers and workstations, especially those accessing sensitive data or critical systems.

Short-Term Actions (Medium Priority)

  • Develop and Implement an Acceptable Use Policy (AUP): Create a comprehensive AUP that outlines acceptable and unacceptable use of company IT resources. Ensure all employees are trained on the policy and acknowledge it.

Long-Term / Ongoing Actions

  • Conduct Regular Vulnerability Scanning: Perform regular, comprehensive vulnerability scans on all external and internal network assets, including any publicly exposed IP addresses and internal server infrastructure. Address identified vulnerabilities promptly.

  • Annual Policy Review and Updates: Establish a schedule for annual review and updates of all security policies and procedures, including the AUP and MFA requirements, to ensure they remain current and effective.

  • Continuous Security Awareness Training: Continue and expand security awareness training programs, covering topics like phishing, social engineering, and secure browsing practices, for all employees on an ongoing basis.
