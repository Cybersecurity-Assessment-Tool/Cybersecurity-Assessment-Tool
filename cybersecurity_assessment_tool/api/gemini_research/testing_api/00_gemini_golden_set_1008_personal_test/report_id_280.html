Recommendations

To mitigate the identified risks and enhance the overall cybersecurity posture of Apex Legends Group, the following actionable recommendations are provided:

  1. Mandate Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for Email Access:

    • Action: Implement and enforce MFA for all organizational email accounts. This should be a top priority.

    • Benefit: Provides a critical layer of defense against credential theft, significantly reducing the risk of email account compromise and associated attacks (e.g., phishing, BEC).

    • Priority: Critical

  2. Develop and Implement a Formal Acceptable Use Policy (AUP):

    • Action: Create a comprehensive AUP that clearly outlines employee responsibilities and acceptable practices for using company IT assets, networks, and data. Ensure all employees read, understand, and acknowledge the policy.

    • Benefit: Establishes clear boundaries, reduces risky behavior, minimizes legal and compliance exposure, and fosters a more secure operational environment.

    • Priority: High

  3. Establish a Regular Security Awareness Training Program:

    • Action: Implement a mandatory annual security awareness training program for all employees, supplementing the initial training for new hires. The training should cover current threats, organizational policies, and best practices.

    • Benefit: Ensures employees maintain up-to-date knowledge on cybersecurity threats and best practices, empowering them to act as a stronger line of defense against social engineering and other attacks.

    • Priority: High

  4. Conduct Periodic, Comprehensive Network and Vulnerability Assessments:

    • Action: While the initial scan showed no open ports, regularly schedule and conduct more comprehensive network vulnerability assessments (including authenticated scans for internal systems) to identify potential misconfigurations, newly introduced services, or software vulnerabilities.

    • Benefit: Proactively identifies and addresses security weaknesses as the IT environment evolves, preventing new attack vectors from emerging.

    • Priority: Medium

Conclusion

Apex Legends Group currently benefits from a technically sound foundation, as evidenced by the lack of exposed services on the scanned host. However, the identified gaps in security policies and user awareness, particularly the absence of MFA for email and comprehensive annual training, represent significant areas for improvement. By proactively implementing the recommended controls and policies, Apex Legends Group can significantly enhance its cybersecurity posture, reduce its attack surface, and build a more resilient defense against the ever-evolving threat landscape.
