6. Recommendations

To address the identified risks and further enhance the organization’s cybersecurity posture, the following recommendations are provided:

  1. Develop and Implement an Employee Acceptable Use Policy (AUP):

    • Create a comprehensive AUP that clearly outlines employee responsibilities for using organizational IT resources, data handling, and internet/email usage.

    • Ensure all employees review, understand, and formally acknowledge the AUP upon hiring and annually thereafter.

    • Integrate AUP compliance into employee performance reviews and disciplinary actions.

  2. Establish and Enforce Mandatory Annual Security Awareness Training:

    • Implement a robust security awareness training program for all employees, conducted at least once per year.

    • Training content should be current, engaging, and cover topics such as phishing, social engineering, password hygiene, data privacy, incident reporting procedures, and AUP adherence.

    • Use simulated phishing campaigns to test employee vigilance and provide targeted remediation.

  3. Expand Network Scan Scope and Detail:

    • Conduct more comprehensive network scans, including authenticated scans, targeting specific internal and external assets.

    • Ensure future scans identify services, products, and versions to detect outdated software and known vulnerabilities.

    • Investigate the possibility of an internal scan to uncover misconfigurations or vulnerabilities within the internal network that are not exposed externally.

7. Conclusion

[Organization Name] demonstrates a foundational commitment to security through its widespread implementation of MFA. However, critical vulnerabilities exist in its human and policy frameworks. By proactively addressing the absence of an AUP and annual security awareness training, the organization can significantly strengthen its defenses against common and evolving cyber threats. Furthermore, expanding the scope of technical assessments will provide a more complete picture of its overall security posture.
