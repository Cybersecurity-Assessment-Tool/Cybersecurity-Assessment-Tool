Recommendations

Based on the identified risks and control gaps, the following recommendations are provided to enhance [Organization Name]’s cybersecurity posture.

Identity and Access Management

  • Implement Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for Email:

    • Mandate MFA for all user accounts accessing organizational email. This significantly reduces the risk of account compromise from phishing and credential theft.

    • Evaluate and implement strong authentication methods (e.g., FIDO2 security keys, authenticator apps) over weaker forms like SMS-based MFA.

Policy and Governance

  • Develop and Enforce an Acceptable Use Policy (AUP):

    • Create a clear and comprehensive AUP that outlines acceptable and unacceptable use of company IT resources, data, and internet access.

    • Ensure all employees are required to read, understand, and formally acknowledge the AUP.

    • Regularly review and update the AUP to reflect changes in technology and business operations.

Security Awareness and Training

  • Establish Comprehensive Security Awareness Training Program:

    • Mandatory New Employee Training: Implement mandatory security awareness training as part of the onboarding process for all new employees.

    • Annual Refresher Training: Conduct annual or bi-annual security awareness training for all existing employees to reinforce best practices and address emerging threats.

    • Phishing Simulations: Regularly conduct simulated phishing campaigns to test employee vigilance and provide targeted training based on results.

    • Topical Training: Provide ad-hoc training on specific threats (e.g., ransomware, social engineering) as they become prevalent.

General Security Practices

  • Conduct Regular Network Vulnerability Scans:

    • Implement a schedule for regular internal and external network vulnerability scans to identify open ports, services, and software vulnerabilities.

    • Prioritize and remediate identified vulnerabilities based on severity and potential impact.

  • Maintain a Vulnerability Management Program:

    • Establish a formal process for identifying, assessing, tracking, and remediating vulnerabilities across all systems and applications.

    • Ensure timely patching and configuration management.
