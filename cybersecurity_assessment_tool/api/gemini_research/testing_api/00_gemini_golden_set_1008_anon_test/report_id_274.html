Recommendations

To address the identified gaps and strengthen the overall cybersecurity posture of [Organization Name], the following recommendations are provided:

  1. Implement MFA for Sensitive Data Systems:

    • Action: Mandate and deploy Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all systems that process, store, or transmit sensitive organizational or customer data.

    • Benefit: Significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access even if primary credentials are compromised.

    • Priority: Critical

  2. Develop and Enforce an Employee Acceptable Use Policy (AUP):

    • Action: Create a clear, comprehensive, and legally reviewed AUP that outlines acceptable and unacceptable use of company IT resources. Ensure all employees read, understand, and sign the AUP.

    • Benefit: Establishes clear expectations for employee conduct, reduces misuse of resources, and provides a basis for disciplinary action if policies are violated.

    • Priority: High

  3. Institute Mandatory Security Awareness Training for New Employees:

    • Action: Develop and implement an onboarding program that includes mandatory security awareness training for all new hires before they gain full network access.

    • Benefit: Equips new employees with essential knowledge to identify and report threats, significantly reducing the risk of human-factor incidents like phishing or social engineering.

    • Priority: High

  4. Continuous Vulnerability Management:

    • Action: Despite the lack of findings from this limited scan, regularly schedule and conduct authenticated vulnerability scans and penetration tests on all internet-facing assets and internal infrastructure.

    • Benefit: Proactively identifies and remediates technical vulnerabilities before they can be exploited by attackers.

    • Priority: Medium (Ongoing)

  5. Review and Update Existing Security Policies:

    • Action: Periodically review and update all security policies, including the acceptable use policy, data handling policies, and incident response plans, to ensure they remain relevant and effective against evolving threats.

    • Benefit: Maintains a robust and adaptive security posture.

    • Priority: Medium (Ongoing)
