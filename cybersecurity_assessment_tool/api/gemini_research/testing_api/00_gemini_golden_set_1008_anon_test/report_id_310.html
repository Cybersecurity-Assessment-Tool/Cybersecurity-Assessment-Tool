Recommendations

To address the identified risks and improve the overall cybersecurity posture of [Organization Name], the following recommendations are provided:

  1. Implement Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for Email:

    • Action: Mandate MFA for all email accounts across the organization.

    • Benefit: Significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access to email accounts even if passwords are compromised, preventing phishing and BEC attacks.

  2. Develop and Implement an Employee Acceptable Use Policy (AUP):

    • Action: Draft, approve, and disseminate a comprehensive AUP outlining the proper use of company IT resources, internet, email, and devices.

    • Benefit: Establishes clear expectations for employee conduct, reduces risky behavior, and helps protect the organization from malware, data leakage, and legal repercussions.

  3. Implement Mandatory Annual Security Awareness Training for All Employees:

    • Action: Establish a program for mandatory annual security awareness training for all employees, not just new hires, covering current threats like phishing, ransomware, and social engineering.

    • Benefit: Ensures that all employees are regularly educated on evolving cyber threats and best practices, strengthening the human firewall against attacks.

  4. Conduct Regular Network Vulnerability Scans:

    • Action: Implement a schedule for periodic network vulnerability scanning of all internal and external assets.

    • Benefit: Proactively identifies technical weaknesses, misconfigurations, and outdated software that could be exploited by attackers.

  5. Consider a Comprehensive Risk Assessment:

    • Action: Engage in a more thorough risk assessment that includes in-depth technical penetration testing, policy review, and compliance auditing.

    • Benefit: Provides a deeper understanding of the organization’s risk landscape, uncovering additional vulnerabilities and compliance gaps beyond the scope of this initial assessment.

Implementing these recommendations will significantly enhance [Organization Name]’s security posture and resilience against modern cyber threats.
