3.1 Analysis of Questionnaire

The organization has robust MFA enforcement and an AUP, which are foundational security practices. However, a significant critical gap exists in security awareness training. The lack of training for new employees means they are immediately vulnerable to social engineering attacks upon joining. The absence of annual training for all employees leaves the entire workforce susceptible to evolving threats like phishing, malware, and social engineering over time. This represents a substantial human factor risk.

6. Recommendations

To address the identified risks and enhance the overall cybersecurity posture, the following recommendations are provided:

  1. Implement Mandatory New Employee Security Awareness Training: Develop and implement a comprehensive security awareness training program for all new hires. This training should be a mandatory component of the onboarding process, covering topics such as phishing, malware, password hygiene, social engineering, and acceptable use policies.

  2. Establish Annual Security Awareness Training for All Employees: Conduct mandatory, annual security awareness training for all employees. This training should be engaging, up-to-date with current threat landscapes, and include simulated phishing exercises to reinforce learning.

  3. Regularly Review and Update Training Content: Ensure that security awareness training materials are regularly reviewed and updated to reflect new threats, organizational policy changes, and best practices.

  4. Monitor and Evaluate Training Effectiveness: Implement metrics to track participation and effectiveness of security awareness training (e.g., click rates on simulated phishing emails before and after training).

  5. Conduct Regular External Network Scans: If not already performing, schedule and conduct regular external vulnerability scans (e.g., quarterly) against public-facing IP addresses to identify any inadvertently exposed services or misconfigurations. Ensure comprehensive scan data is collected and analyzed for proactive risk management.
