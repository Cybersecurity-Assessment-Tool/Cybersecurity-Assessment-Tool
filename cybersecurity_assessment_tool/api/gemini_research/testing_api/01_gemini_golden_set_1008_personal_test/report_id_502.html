Recommendations

Based on the findings of this assessment, the following actions are recommended to mitigate the identified risks and improve the overall security posture of Blue Marble.

High Priority Recommendations

RISK-001: Implement MFA

Action: Deploy a mandatory Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) solution for all employees and contractors. Prioritize implementation in the following order:

  1. Remote access systems (VPN, RDP).

  2. Email (e.g., Office 365, Google Workspace).

  3. Access to systems containing sensitive or critical data.

  4. General computer and system logins.

Impact: Drastically reduces the risk of account compromise via stolen credentials.

RISK-002: Enhance Onboarding Process

Action: Integrate mandatory cybersecurity awareness training into the standard new employee onboarding process. This training should be completed within the first week of employment and cover topics such as phishing, password hygiene, and the acceptable use policy. Impact: Reduces the likelihood of new employees falling victim to common cyber attacks and establishes a security-conscious culture from day one.
