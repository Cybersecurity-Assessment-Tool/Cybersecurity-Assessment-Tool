Recommendations

To enhance the cybersecurity posture of Opal Sky Media, the following actionable recommendations are provided:

  • Implement MFA for Sensitive Data Systems: Immediately implement and enforce Multi-Factor Authentication for all systems accessing or storing sensitive organizational data. This will significantly reduce the risk of credential compromise leading to data breaches.

  • Regular Security Control Review: Conduct periodic reviews of all security controls, especially those related to data access and employee training, to ensure they remain effective and align with current best practices and threat landscapes.

  • Periodic Vulnerability Scanning: Establish a schedule for regular internal and external vulnerability scanning to continuously monitor for open ports, outdated software, and misconfigurations. This helps in early detection and validation of mitigation efforts.

  • Maintain Documentation of Risk Mitigation: For risks like the "Unencrypted Web Server," ensure that the mitigation steps (e.g., closing the port, implementing SSL redirection) are formally documented and routinely verified to confirm their ongoing effectiveness.

  • Incident Response Planning: Ensure an up-to-date incident response plan is in place and regularly tested through tabletop exercises or simulations to address potential security incidents efficiently and minimize impact.

Conclusion

Opal Sky Media demonstrates a commendable commitment to several foundational security practices, including widespread MFA adoption for email and computer logins, and consistent security awareness training. However, the absence of MFA for sensitive data systems presents a critical vulnerability that requires immediate attention. The recent network scan indicates a positive development with the closure of Port 80, potentially mitigating a previously identified risk. By addressing the identified gaps and diligently implementing the recommended enhancements, Opal Sky Media can significantly strengthen its overall cybersecurity defenses and resilience against evolving threats.
