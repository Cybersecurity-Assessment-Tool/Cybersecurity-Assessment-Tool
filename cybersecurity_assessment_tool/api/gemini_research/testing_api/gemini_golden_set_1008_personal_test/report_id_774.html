Recommendations

To address the identified risks and enhance the overall cybersecurity posture of Structure & Form, the following actionable recommendations are provided:

Policy and Training Enhancements

  1. Develop and Implement an Employee Acceptable Use Policy (AUP): Create a comprehensive AUP that clearly outlines employee responsibilities, acceptable use of company IT resources (e.g., internet, email, software), data handling procedures, and security protocols. Ensure this policy is communicated to all employees, and obtain documented acknowledgment of understanding and compliance.

  2. Establish Mandatory Annual Security Awareness Training for All Employees: Implement a regular, mandatory security awareness training program for all employees, not just new hires. This training should be conducted at least once per year and cover current threats such as phishing, ransomware, social engineering, password best practices, and incident reporting procedures. Track employee participation and completion rates.

Technical and Process Improvements

  1. Maintain a Minimal Attack Surface: Continue the practice of ensuring that only strictly necessary ports and services are exposed to internal and external networks. Regularly review and audit firewall rules, network configurations, and exposed services to proactively close or secure any unnecessary access points.

  2. Conduct Regular Security Assessments: Supplement internal reviews with periodic external vulnerability assessments and penetration tests. This proactive approach helps to identify and address security weaknesses from an attacker’s perspective before they can be exploited.

  3. Maintain an Accurate Risk Register: Implement a process to regularly review and update the organization’s risk register. Ensure that all identified risks, including those from previous assessments, are accurately documented with their current status (e.g., open, mitigated, closed) based on the latest assessments and remedial actions.
