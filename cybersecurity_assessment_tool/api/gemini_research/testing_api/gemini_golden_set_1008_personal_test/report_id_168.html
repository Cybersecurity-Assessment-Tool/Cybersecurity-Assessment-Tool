Executive Summary

It is important to note that the network scan data and pre-existing vulnerability information provided for this assessment were incomplete or unparseable. Therefore, this report’s technical findings are limited, and the risk assessment is primarily derived from the organizational questionnaire. A comprehensive understanding of the organization’s external attack surface and active threats requires successful technical scans and a complete risk register.

Recommendations are provided to address the identified gaps and improve the overall security posture, focusing on immediate control enhancements and broader risk management practices.

Recommendations

To mitigate the identified risks and enhance the overall cybersecurity posture of Fable & Lore, the following recommendations are provided:

Immediate Actions

  1. Implement MFA for All Sensitive Data Systems (Risk R-001):

    • Action: Mandate and enforce multi-factor authentication for all systems containing or accessing sensitive organizational data.

    • Rationale: This significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access even if primary credentials are compromised, aligning with industry best practices for data protection.

    • Priority: Critical

  2. Establish Annual Security Awareness Training for All Employees (Risk R-002):

    • Action: Develop and implement a mandatory annual security awareness training program for all employees, supplementing the existing new-hire training.

    • Rationale: Regular training ensures that all personnel are updated on the latest threats (e.g., phishing, malware, social engineering) and best practices, fostering a stronger security culture and reducing human-error related incidents.

    • Priority: High

General Security Enhancements

  1. Conduct Comprehensive Network Vulnerability Scans:

    • Action: Schedule and execute regular, comprehensive network vulnerability scans against all external and internal assets. Ensure the scan results are parseable and thoroughly analyzed.

    • Rationale: This provides critical insights into the actual technical attack surface, identifies exploitable vulnerabilities, and helps prioritize patching and remediation efforts.

    • Priority: High

  2. Maintain a Centralized Risk Register:

    • Action: Establish a formal process for identifying, documenting, assessing, and tracking all cybersecurity risks and vulnerabilities in a centralized risk register.

    • Rationale: A well-maintained risk register enables proactive risk management, facilitates informed decision-making, and provides a clear overview of the organization’s risk landscape.

    • Priority: Medium

  3. Regular Security Posture Review:

    • Action: Implement a periodic review (e.g., quarterly or bi-annually) of security controls, policies, and incident response plans to adapt to changing threat landscapes and business requirements.

    • Rationale: Cybersecurity is an evolving field; regular reviews ensure that the organization’s defenses remain effective and resilient against new threats.

    • Priority: Medium

Conclusion

While Fable & Lore has some foundational security measures in place, the assessment highlights significant areas for improvement, particularly concerning MFA for sensitive data and ongoing security awareness training. The limitations imposed by incomplete technical scan data and pre-existing risk information underscore the necessity for robust data collection and comprehensive assessment methodologies. Addressing the identified recommendations will substantially enhance the organization’s resilience against common cyber threats and foster a more mature security posture.
